Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to use?

Yes, you can download and use Nails A2Z mobile apps forever free on Apple and Google app stores.

How can I post nail jobs or salons?

First, you need to create a free Nails A2Z account on the app. After that, you'll be able to upload your nail jobs, tech profiles, or salons for sale. The free account also allows you to place orders or join nail classes.

How can I sell my products?

If you want to upload and sell your products on Nails A2Z and get paid, please contact us to open a free Seller account. We'll dedicate a sales rep to help you with the onboarding process. Also let us know if you need to advertise your nail courses or events on our platform.

How can I pay for my orders?

Currently, we only accept major credit or debit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express.

Is there a web version of the app?

We'll be releasing the web version of our app soon with full features and data like the mobile apps.
